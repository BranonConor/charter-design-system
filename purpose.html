Purpose & Vision

The Charter Design System

At Charter, our healthcare providers are ultimately focused on positively impacting their patients and providing the best possible healthcare experience. In the modern age of technology and internet, we want to ensure that we are also providing the best possible online Charter experience to extend our positive impact into the digital sphere. This design system helps us accomplish just that - by unifying our digital design and making sure it properly communicates our company's values, we can create digital products that impact our patients and others just as powerfully as our healthcare initiatives.
